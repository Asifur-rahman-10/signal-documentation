# Getting Started

## Install TestSignal to your Shopify Store.

If you haven’t already, install TestSignal from the [Shopify App Store](http://admin.shopify.com/store).<br />
Once installed, you can begin running experiments on product price, shipping, store themes, images, descriptions, and layouts.

## What you can do right away

TestSignal empowers you to test almost any element of your Shopify storefront — from pricing and shipping to visual content and layout — so you can optimize based on data, not guesswork.
**Here’s how to get up and running in minutes**

- **Install TestSignal** from the [Shopify App Store](http://admin.shopify.com/store)
- **Approve billing** to unlock your 14-day free trial on our Core Plan — cancel anytime. Learn more [Here](http://admin.shopify.com/store)
- **Enable the TestSignal extension** in Shopify under Online Store → Customize → App Embeds and save
- **Choose Your Experiment** Pick Price, Shipping, Product Images, Descriptions, Themes, or Layouts
- **Create your first test,** it only takes a few minutes
- **Launch and watch** your experiment in action with real-time results
